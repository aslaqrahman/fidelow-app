Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Fidelow?
Fidelow is a personal CRM built specifically for solo founders, freelancers, and creators. Unlike traditional CRMs (like Salesforce or Hubspot) which focus on sales pipelines and closing deals, Fidelow focuses on nurturing authentic relationships over time.
Who is it for?
It's for anyone whose network is their net worth, but who doesn't have a dedicated sales team. If you find yourself forgetting details about people you met, missing follow-ups, or losing track of your conversations, Fidelow is for you.
Can I import my existing contacts?
Yes! Upon launch, Fidelow will support CSV imports, as well as easy integrations with Google Contacts and Apple Contacts to help you get started in seconds.
Will there be AI features?
Yes. We use AI thoughtfully—not to generate fake personalized emails, but to help you process information. Our core AI feature helps summarize your messy post-meeting voice notes into structured profiles and reminders.
When will it launch and is it free?
We are currently in private beta. Join the waitlist below to get early access. Pricing will reflect a premium, ad-free, data-private tool, with a free tier available for early-stage founders.
